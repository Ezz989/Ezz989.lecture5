Here you can read more about the degree we're pursuing:

During the program, you will learn to devise and implement new inventions that we will need, both in our daily lives and on a larger scale. This could be software for airplanes, self-driving cars or systems such as e-boxes. Programming is a fundamental part of the program, and database systems, embedded systems, interaction technologies and networks will become part of your daily life. With a bachelor's degree in software, you can continue on to a master's degree in software and become a civil engineer. You will then typically have job opportunities in companies that develop and produce software and IT solutions.
